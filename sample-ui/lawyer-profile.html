About

Adv. A. Sharma is an independent advocate with a result-oriented, ethical approach to family and matrimonial law. Over 18 years of practice, she has advised and represented clients across divorce, child custody, maintenance, and domestic-violence matters.

She regularly appears before the family courts and the Karnataka High Court, and is known for clear, practical advice and strong client communication. She is enrolled with the Bar Council of Karnataka.
